About Me

Thakane Juanita Eiman is a highly motivated and detail-oriented Information Technology student with a strong foundation in web development, software design, and project management. With a passion for coding and technology, she has completed several training programs, including SheCodes Web Development, SheCodes Plus, and an ongoing React course. In addition, she is pursuing a Diploma in Information Technology at the University of South Africa, equipping herself with essential programming and problem-solving skills. Her technical expertise spans HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Visual Basic, and Android Studio, with a keen interest in software development and user-centered design.

Before transitioning to IT, Thakane spent over a decade in the construction industry as a Finishing Site Supervisor at Group 5 Structured Ingenuity. Her experience in project documentation, stakeholder communication, and quality control has provided her with valuable transferable skills such as analytical thinking, problem-solving, and attention to detail. She successfully managed subcontractors, ensured project timelines were met, and led quality inspections, all of which highlight her ability to work under pressure and deliver results. Her background in project management, strengthened by a certification from Coventry University Online, complements her growing expertise in software development.

Currently, Thakane is actively building her portfolio by working on personal web development projects and expanding her knowledge in front-end and back-end technologies. She is proficient in Git and GitHub for version control and has a strong grasp of IT applications used in project tracking and construction management. As she seeks an entry-level position in the IT industry, she is eager to apply her technical and problem-solving skills in a professional environment, continuously learn, and contribute meaningfully to innovative projects. Through dedication, perseverance, and a passion for technology, Thakane aims to make a lasting impact in the field of software development.
